Security

How we protect your data and systems

Our Commitment

Security is foundational to everything we build. When you trust us with your business workflows, data, and systems, we take that responsibility seriously. Here's how we protect what matters.

Infrastructure Security

🔒

Encryption in Transit

All data transmitted between your browser and our services is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ (SSL). Every page on our site is served over HTTPS.

☁️

Secure Cloud Hosting

Our website and services are hosted on Vercel's enterprise-grade infrastructure, which includes automatic DDoS protection, edge caching, and SOC 2 compliance.

🔑

Access Controls

Access to client systems and data is restricted to authorized team members only. We use role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication across all internal tools.

Client Data Protection

🛡️

Data Isolation

Each client's systems, automations, and data are fully isolated. No client can access another client's information, configurations, or workflows.

📋

Minimal Data Collection

We only collect the information necessary to deliver our services. Form submissions are transmitted securely and are not stored on our web servers.

🗑️

Data Retention

Client data is retained only for the duration of the engagement plus a reasonable period for support purposes. Upon request, we will delete all client data from our systems.

Operational Security

  • All team members undergo security awareness training.
  • We conduct regular reviews of access permissions and revoke access promptly when no longer needed.
  • Third-party integrations are vetted for security practices before being incorporated into client systems.
  • We follow the principle of least privilege — team members only have access to what they need for their role.

Vulnerability Reporting

If you discover a security vulnerability on our website or in any system we've built, please report it responsibly through our Contact page. We take all reports seriously and will respond promptly.
