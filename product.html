Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Your AI Edge.

What exactly is Your AI Edge?
Your AI Edge is an AI operating system for founders. It manages your inbox, calendar, CRM, content, and operations — autonomously, in your voice. Think of it as a chief of staff that works 24/7.
How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?
ChatGPT answers questions. Edge does work. It's connected to your email, calendar, and tools — and takes action autonomously. It drafts emails, books meetings, follows up with leads, and creates content. You approve, Edge executes.
Is my data safe?
Yes. AES-256 encryption, SOC 2 Type II compliant, GDPR compliant. Your data is never sold or used for training. Every action can require your approval.
Can Edge actually send emails on my behalf?
Yes — but only with your approval. Edge drafts emails in your voice, then waits for your thumbs up. You can also enable auto-send for low-risk responses.
What tools does Edge integrate with?
Gmail, Google Calendar, Slack, Notion, HubSpot, Salesforce, Stripe, Zoom, Teams, Calendly, Zapier, and 20+ more. If your tool has an API, we can connect it.
How long before Edge sounds like me?
Edge starts learning from day one by analyzing your sent emails. Within a week, drafts are close. Within a month, they're indistinguishable.
Can I text Edge from my phone?
Yes. iMessage, SMS, WhatsApp, Telegram, and Slack are all supported. Text Edge like you'd text a friend.
What's the White-Glove service?
White-Glove includes everything in Edge plus a dedicated account manager, custom workflow design, weekly briefs, revenue monitoring, and monthly strategic reviews.
How much does it cost?
Edge starts at $99/month for founders. White-Glove is $499/month. Both include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
What if Edge makes a mistake?
Approval mode means nothing goes out without your OK. Edge also learns from corrections — every edit makes it smarter.
Can I use Edge for my team?
Yes. Team plans are available. Each member gets their own Edge with shared context for the company.
How do I get started?
Sign up at youredge.ai, connect your email and calendar, and Edge starts working in under 60 seconds. It's that simple.
